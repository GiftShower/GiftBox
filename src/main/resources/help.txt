Commands:
    `<prefix>ping: Pong!`
    `<prefix>info: Gets Information about this server.`
    `<prefix>setprefix [arg]: Changes current prefix to the argument given.`

Music Specific:
    `<prefix>join: Joins to the voice channel.`
    `<prefix>play [arg]: Plays the first search result from the given argument.`
    `<prefix>stop: Stops playing.`
    `<prefix>np: Gets the information of the song currently playing.`
    `<prefix>leave: Goodbye.`
